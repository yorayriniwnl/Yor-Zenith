ZENITH — SERVICE 1
SOLAR FEASIBILITY & BENEFIT ANALYSIS (FINAL MATH — REALISTIC)

--------------------------------------------------
INPUTS
--------------------------------------------------
- Monthly electricity consumption (kWh/month)
- Monthly electricity bill (₹/month)
- Location-based Feasibility Score (FS), where FS ∈ [1, 10]

--------------------------------------------------
ASSUMPTIONS
--------------------------------------------------
- 1 month ≈ 30 days
- Under ideal conditions (FS = 10), 1 kW solar generates ≈ 4 kWh/day
- Real-world system losses ≈ 20% (inverter, wiring, dust, heat)
- Standard solar panel size = 550 W = 0.55 kW
- Installation cost ≈ ₹60,000 per kW
- Solar panel lifetime = 25 years
- Net metering enabled
- Realistic solar offset factor = 80% (OFFSET = 0.8)
- Subsidies assumed 0 for MVP (can be added later)

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: DAILY ENERGY REQUIREMENT
--------------------------------------------------
Convert monthly usage to daily usage:

E_daily (kWh/day) =
Monthly_kWh / 30

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: FEASIBILITY SCORE EFFICIENCY
--------------------------------------------------
Convert FS to efficiency factor:

Efficiency =
FS / 10

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: RAW SYSTEM SIZE (IDEAL CONDITIONS)
--------------------------------------------------
Energy produced per kW per day:

Production_per_kW =
4 × Efficiency

Required system size (ideal):

S_kW_raw =
E_daily / Production_per_kW

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 4: REAL-WORLD PERFORMANCE BUFFER
--------------------------------------------------
Account for real-world system losses (~20%):

S_kW_buffered =
S_kW_raw × 1.2

(This ensures the system meets demand in real conditions)

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 5: NUMBER OF SOLAR PANELS
--------------------------------------------------
Panel capacity:

Panel_size =
0.55 kW

Number of panels (rounded up):

N_panels =
ceil(S_kW_buffered / 0.55)

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 6: ACTUAL INSTALLED CAPACITY
--------------------------------------------------
Installers install whole panels only:

S_actual (kW) =
N_panels × 0.55

(This is the actual system capacity used for cost and ROI)

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 7: TOTAL INSTALLATION COST
--------------------------------------------------
Cost per kW:

Cost_per_kW =
₹60,000

Total project cost:

Cost_total (₹) =
S_actual × 60,000

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 8: ANNUAL SAVINGS (REALISTIC)
--------------------------------------------------
Solar does not offset 100% of the bill due to:
- nighttime usage
- seasonal mismatch
- grid export limits
- consumption patterns

Assume realistic offset factor:

OFFSET =
0.8

Annual savings:

Savings_annual (₹/year) =
Monthly_Bill × 12 × OFFSET

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 9: PAYBACK PERIOD
--------------------------------------------------
Years required to recover installation cost:

Payback_years =
Cost_total / Savings_annual

--------------------------------------------------
STEP 10: LIFETIME PROFIT (25 YEARS)
--------------------------------------------------
Total savings over system lifetime:

Savings_lifetime =
Savings_annual × 25

Lifetime profit:

Profit_lifetime (₹) =
Savings_lifetime − Cost_total

--------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES (USER-FACING EXPLANATION)
--------------------------------------------------
- Solar systems typically offset 70–90% of household electricity costs.
  Zenith uses an 80% offset to reflect realistic usage patterns.

- Excess solar energy generated during high-sunlight months is exported
  to the grid and adjusted against consumption during low-sunlight periods
  under net metering.

- Savings are calculated using current electricity tariffs.
  Actual savings may increase over time as grid electricity prices rise.

--------------------------------------------------
OUTPUTS SHOWN TO USER
--------------------------------------------------
- Feasibility Score (FS)
- Recommended system size (kW)
- Number of solar panels
- Installation cost (₹)
- Annual savings (₹/year)
- Payback period (years)
- 25-year lifetime profit (₹)
- AI-generated explanation (Gemini)
